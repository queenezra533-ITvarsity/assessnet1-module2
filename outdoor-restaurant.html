OUTDOOR RESTAURANT

Not quite indoors, not quite outdoors, open-air eateries are an idyllic place to enjoy the gorgeous weather. Whether it’s midsummer or transitioning into fall, indoor-outdoor restaurants make a weekend brunch or quick cocktail that much more enjoyable. They let in the warm weather while still protecting patrons from harsh direct sun. There really is no better way to enjoy a meal than with a little fresh air. From rooftops to pavilions to dining rooms lined with window walls, we’ve rounded up some of the world’s best alfresco eateries that have excellent architectural and interior design, as well as delectable dishes. Take a closer look at a chic open-faced coffee shop in China or a fine-dining restaurant in Barbados with trees built into its structure. Get ready for these refreshing takes on outdoor dining
